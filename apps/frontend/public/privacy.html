Privacy Policy

Career Autopilot

Last updated:

1. Information We Collect

Career Autopilot is a personal job search automation tool that helps you manage your job applications. We collect the following information:

1.1 LinkedIn Integration

  • Basic profile information (name, email) when you connect your LinkedIn account
  • Job search results and application data
  • No sensitive personal information beyond what's necessary for job searching

1.2 Application Data

  • Job listings you save or apply to
  • Resume and cover letter content
  • Application tracking information

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely for the purpose of:

  • Automating your job search process
  • Tracking your applications and their status
  • Generating personalized resumes and cover letters
  • Providing job recommendations

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored locally on your device and in secure cloud storage. We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information.

Important: This is a personal automation tool. Your data is not shared with third parties or used for commercial purposes.

4. Third-Party Services

We integrate with the following services:

  • LinkedIn: For job search and application data
  • Overleaf: For resume generation (if configured)
  • Email Services: For notifications (if configured)

Each service has its own privacy policy. We only access data you explicitly authorize.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data at any time
  • Delete your data and account
  • Modify or update your information
  • Disconnect third-party integrations

6. Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary for the functionality of the application. You can delete your data at any time through the application settings.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy.

8. Contact Information

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us through the Career Autopilot application or via the GitHub repository.

Note: This privacy policy is for a personal automation tool. No data is collected for commercial purposes or shared with third parties beyond the integrations you explicitly authorize.
